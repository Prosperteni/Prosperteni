About

Prosper Ughakpoteni is an artificial intelligence and robotics developer with over 3 years of professional coding experience. His work focuses on the intersection of machine learning, computer vision, and systems design.

He specializes in building practical systems that solve real-world problems rather than pursuing purely theoretical approaches. This philosophy informs his work across multiple domains:

  • Artificial Intelligence: Transfer learning models, explainable AI, and domain-specific applications
  • Computer Vision: Real-time object detection, image processing, and visual system design
  • Web Systems: Full-stack development with emphasis on clean, maintainable architecture
