About

Prosper Ughakpoteni is an AI and robotics engineer with over 3 years of professional development experience. His work bridges theoretical machine learning research with practical, deployable systems for real-world applications.

With expertise spanning artificial intelligence, computer vision, and autonomous systems, Prosper maintains a philosophy of pragmatic engineering: focusing on interpretable, maintainable solutions over theoretical complexity.

Core Competencies

  • Artificial Intelligence: Transfer learning, explainable AI (SHAP/LIME), model deployment
  • Computer Vision: Real-time object detection (YOLO), image processing, visual system architecture
  • Robotics & Autonomy: Sensor integration, control systems, autonomous decision-making
  • Systems Engineering: Full-stack development, REST APIs, database architecture
